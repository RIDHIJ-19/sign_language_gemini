IndicSign

IndicSign is a gesture recognition system that uses the Gemini API for multimodal understanding to translate Indian Sign Language (ISL) gestures into readable text. Combining computer vision and machine learning, it enables real-time, inclusive communication for the hearing and speech impaired, promoting accessibility and empathy across everyday and educational settings.

"When hands speak, and machines listen — understanding becomes universal."
